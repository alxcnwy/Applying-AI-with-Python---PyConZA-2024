Bio Part 2: Bruce Merry is a senior software developer at the South African Radio Astronomy Observatory (SARAO), developing Python software for the MeerKAT radio telescope. He specialises in high performance computing including networking and GPU computing. He earned his PhD in Computer Science from the University of Cape Town in 2007, after which he spent 3 years at ARM in the UK. In 2011 he returned to South Africa to complete a post-doctoral fellowship at UCT before joining SARAO.
